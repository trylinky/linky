A verified badge shows visitors that a page genuinely belongs to you - useful for reassuring your audience that they're in the right place and not on an impersonator's copy. On Linky, the verification badge is available on the **Premium** plan and appears on your page once enabled.

## Why it matters

- **Trust.** A badge signals authenticity, so people click your links with confidence.
- **Protection.** As you grow, copycat pages become more common; a verified mark helps your real page stand apart.

## How to get one

1. Upgrade to [Premium](/i/pricing) - verification is included alongside custom domains, analytics, and unlimited blocks.
2. Enable the badge on your page from the editor.
3. Publish, and the badge shows next to your name.

Pairing the badge with a [custom domain](/i/learn/can-i-use-my-own-domain) and an on-brand [theme](/i/learn/how-to-customise-your-link-in-bio-page) makes your page look unmistakably official. See everything Premium includes on the [pricing page](/i/pricing).
