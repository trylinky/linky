If your link in bio isn't working, it's almost always one of a few simple causes. Work through these in order:

## Common fixes

- **Check for typos.** Make sure the URL is spelled correctly and includes no stray spaces. Copy and paste it rather than typing it by hand.
- **Include the full address.** Some apps only make a link tappable when it starts with `https://` - paste the complete URL, not just the domain.
- **Save your profile.** On Instagram, TikTok, and similar apps, links only go live after you tap **Save** on the edit screen.
- **Put the link in the right field.** A URL typed into your bio *text* may not be clickable on every platform - use the dedicated **Website** or **Link** field where one exists.
- **Wait a moment, then refresh.** New links and newly published pages can take a minute to appear. Close and reopen the app.
- **Test in a private window.** Open the link in a browser you're not logged into to confirm the page itself loads.

## If your link-in-bio page is the problem

Make sure the page is **published**, not just saved as a draft, and that the username in the URL matches your page exactly. If you use a [custom domain](/i/learn/can-i-use-my-own-domain), check that its DNS is set up and verified.
