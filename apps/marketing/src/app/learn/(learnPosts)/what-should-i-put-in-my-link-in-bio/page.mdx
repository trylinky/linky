A good link-in-bio page leads with whatever you most want people to do right now, then lists your evergreen links below it. Put your single most important call-to-action first - a new release, a product, a sign-up - and keep the rest short and scannable.

## Ideas for what to include

- **Your latest thing.** A new video, song, blog post, or product drop at the very top.
- **Your shop or store.** Link straight to your products or a featured item.
- **A newsletter or waitlist sign-up.** Capture an audience you actually own.
- **Your other socials.** Make it easy to follow you everywhere.
- **Music and video.** A live [Spotify block](/i/integrations/spotify) or your [latest TikTok](/i/integrations/tiktok) keeps the page fresh on its own.
- **Contact or booking.** An email, a booking link, or a "work with me" page.

## Keep it focused

You don't need every link you've ever made - too many choices and people click nothing. Lead with one clear action, group the rest, and reorder them whenever your priorities change. If you're not sure where to start, browse [templates](/i/templates) or see [real examples](/i/explore) from other creators for inspiration.
