The best way to collect emails from your link in bio is to add a sign-up form directly to your page, so visitors can join your list without leaving. With Linky you can add an email capture (waitlist) block that collects addresses right on your page.

An email list is one of the few audiences you actually own - unlike followers, it isn't subject to an algorithm - so putting a sign-up front and centre on your link-in-bio page is one of the highest-value things you can do with it.

## Step by step

1. Open your page in the editor and add an **email sign-up / waitlist** block.
2. Write a short, clear prompt - tell people what they'll get for subscribing.
3. Place it near the top of your page so it's the first thing people see.
4. Publish, and export or sync the addresses you collect.

## Tips for more sign-ups

- Offer a reason to subscribe: early access, a freebie, or behind-the-scenes updates.
- Keep the form to a single field (just the email) to reduce friction.
- Drive traffic to it from your stories and posts using your [link in bio](/i/learn/how-to-add-a-link-to-your-instagram-story).
