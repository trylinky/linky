To add a link on Snapchat, you'll use your **Public Profile**. Open your profile, tap to edit it, and add a website link in the available field; you can also attach links to individual Spotlight posts and Stories so viewers can swipe up to visit them.

Snapchat's linking options are limited and change often, so the most reliable approach is to share one link-in-bio URL. It opens a page with everything you want people to see - and you can update that page without editing Snapchat at all.

## Step by step

1. Create a free link-in-bio page and add your links and content.
2. Copy your URL, e.g. `lin.ky/yourname`.
3. Add it to your Snapchat Public Profile and attach it to Stories or Spotlight posts where relevant.

Using one link-in-bio across Snapchat, Instagram, and TikTok means a single place to manage everything. You can get started for [free](/i/pricing) and make it yours with a [template](/i/templates).
