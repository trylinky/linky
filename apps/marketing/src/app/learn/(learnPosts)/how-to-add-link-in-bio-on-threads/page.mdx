To add a link on Threads, open your profile, tap **Edit profile**, select **Link**, and paste your URL. It appears as a tappable link under your bio. Threads also makes links you include in individual posts clickable.

Threads shows a single profile link, so if you want to point followers to several places, use a link-in-bio page as that one link. It opens a tidy hub of everything you share, and you only update the page - never your Threads profile.

## Step by step

1. Create a free link-in-bio page and add your links and blocks.
2. Copy your URL, e.g. `lin.ky/yourname`.
3. On Threads, go to **Edit profile → Link** and paste it in.

If you want your page to react to your activity, Linky can show a live [Threads follower count](/i/integrations/threads) and other [integrations](/i/integrations) alongside your links. It's [free to start](/i/pricing).
