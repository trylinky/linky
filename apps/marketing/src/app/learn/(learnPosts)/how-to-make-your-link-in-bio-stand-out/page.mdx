To make your link in bio stand out, give it a look and content that feel distinctly yours - most pages are forgettable because they use the same default template with a plain list of links. A little personality and some live content go a long way.

## Make it look like you

- **Own your colours and fonts.** [Customise the theme](/i/learn/how-to-customise-your-link-in-bio-page) to match your brand instead of leaving the default.
- **Add your face.** A clear profile photo and a short, specific bio build instant recognition and trust.
- **Use a [custom domain](/i/learn/can-i-use-my-own-domain)** so your URL reads as `yourbrand.com` rather than a generic short link.

## Make it feel alive

Static link buttons are easy to scroll past. Add [live blocks](/i/integrations) that show real, current activity - what you're [listening to on Spotify](/i/learn/how-to-add-spotify-to-your-link-in-bio), your latest post, your commit streak - so the page changes on its own and rewards repeat visits.

## Make it effortless to act on

Lead with one clear, well-labelled action and keep the rest tidy beneath it. A page that's beautiful *and* easy to use is what truly stands out. Browse [templates](/i/templates) or get inspired by [real pages](/i/explore), then build yours for [free](/i/pricing).
