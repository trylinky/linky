To customise your link-in-bio page, change its theme - the colours, fonts, and backgrounds - and arrange your blocks so the most important ones come first. With Linky you start from a theme and adjust every colour role to match your brand, then drag your blocks into the order you want.

A page that looks like *you* - rather than a generic default - builds trust and makes people more likely to click.

## What you can change

- **Colours.** Adjust the page background, card colours, borders, and text to fit your brand palette.
- **Fonts and feel.** Pick a style that suits your audience, from clean and minimal to bold and colourful.
- **Layout and order.** Put your top priority - a release, a product, a sign-up - at the very top, and group the rest.
- **Profile.** Add your photo, name, and a short bio so the page feels personal.

## The fastest way to start

Browse the [templates](/i/templates) and pick one close to the look you want - each is fully editable, so you only tweak from there. You can also see how other creators set theirs up in [explore](/i/explore). When you're happy, publish and share your link. It's [free](/i/pricing) to build and customise.
