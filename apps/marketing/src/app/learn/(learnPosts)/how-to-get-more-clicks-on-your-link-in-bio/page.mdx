To get more clicks on your link in bio, reduce friction and make the most important link impossible to miss. Most pages lose clicks because they offer too many choices or bury the good stuff halfway down.

## Improve your click-through rate

- **Lead with one priority.** Put your single most important link at the very top. Every extra option above it competes for the same tap.
- **Write action-led labels.** "Shop the new collection" beats "My store". Tell people what happens when they click.
- **Cut the clutter.** Remove dead or low-value links. A short, focused page converts better than a long one.
- **Make it visual.** A thumbnail, cover image, or [live block](/i/integrations) draws the eye far more than a plain text link.
- **Match your brand.** A page that looks polished and trustworthy gets more taps than a generic default - [customise the colours and fonts](/i/learn/how-to-customise-your-link-in-bio-page).

## Measure, then refine

Turn on [analytics](/i/learn/how-to-track-link-in-bio-clicks) and watch which links get clicked. Move winners up, drop the rest, and test new labels. Small changes to order and wording often lift clicks more than a full redesign. Start free with a [template](/i/templates) built to convert.
