Update the top of your link in bio every time your priority changes - a new release, product, video, or campaign. The rest of the page can stay put. As a rule of thumb, refresh the featured spot at least as often as you post important content, and review the whole page once a month.

## What to change, and when

- **Every new launch:** swap the top link to whatever you're promoting right now.
- **Weekly-ish:** check that your most recent content is reachable in a tap.
- **Monthly:** prune dead links, fix anything broken, and re-order based on what's getting clicks.

## Let parts of it update themselves

The easiest way to keep a page current is to use [live blocks](/i/integrations) that refresh on their own - a [Spotify "now playing" block](/i/learn/how-to-add-spotify-to-your-link-in-bio), your [latest Instagram post](/i/integrations/instagram), or your [TikTok](/i/integrations/tiktok). They keep the page feeling alive without any manual work, so you only update the parts that need a human decision.
