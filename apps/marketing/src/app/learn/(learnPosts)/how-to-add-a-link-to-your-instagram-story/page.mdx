To add a link to an Instagram story, create your story, tap the **sticker** icon at the top, choose the **Link** sticker, paste your URL, and place it on your story. Viewers tap the sticker to open the link. Every account can do this - you no longer need 10,000 followers or the old "swipe up" feature.

A handy trick: point the link sticker at your link-in-bio page rather than a single destination. That way one sticker gives viewers access to everything - your shop, your latest drop, your newsletter - and you can keep reusing the same link across stories without editing anything.

## Step by step

1. Tap your **link-in-bio** URL to copy it (e.g. `lin.ky/yourname`).
2. Start a story, tap the **sticker** icon, and choose **Link**.
3. Paste your URL, customise the sticker text if you like, and post.

You can also keep the same link permanently in your bio's [Website field](/i/learn/how-to-add-link-in-bio-to-instagram). Build your page for [free](/i/pricing) and add live blocks so it always shows your latest content.
