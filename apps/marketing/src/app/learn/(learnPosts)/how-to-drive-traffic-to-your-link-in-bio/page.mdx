The most reliable way to drive traffic to your link in bio is to mention it often and give people a reason to tap it. Your link sits quietly in your profile, so it only gets clicks when your content points to it.

## Tactics that work

- **Add a clear call to action.** End posts, captions, and videos with "link in bio" and say *what* they'll find there ("new track in bio", not just "link in bio").
- **Use story link stickers.** Add a [link sticker to your Instagram stories](/i/learn/how-to-add-a-link-to-your-instagram-story) whenever you have something to share.
- **Pin a post** about your link to the top of your profile or feed.
- **Put it everywhere.** Your YouTube description, email signature, podcast show notes, and other bios should all use the same link.
- **Lead with one thing.** Make the top of your page a single, obvious action so the click pays off immediately.

## Then double down on what works
