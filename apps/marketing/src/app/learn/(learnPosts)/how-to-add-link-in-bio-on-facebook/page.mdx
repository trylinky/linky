To add a link to a Facebook **profile**, go to your profile, tap **Edit profile**, scroll to **Bio** or **Links**, and add your URL there. For a Facebook **Page**, open **Edit Page info** (or **About**), add your link in the **Website** field, and consider adding a call-to-action button so visitors have a clear next step.

As with most social platforms, Facebook gives you very little room for links. If you want to share more than one destination, the cleanest approach is to add a single link-in-bio URL - it opens a page that holds all of your links and content in one place, and you can update it without editing your Facebook profile or Page again.

## Step by step

1. Build a free link-in-bio page and add a block for each link you want to share.
2. Publish it and copy your URL (for example, `lin.ky/yourname`).
3. On Facebook, open **Edit profile** (or **Edit Page info**) and paste it into the bio or website field.
4. Save.

A link-in-bio page also works everywhere else you have a bio - Instagram, TikTok, X, and YouTube - so you only maintain one link across every platform. You can start for [free](/i/pricing) and choose a [template](/i/templates) to match your brand.
