You can make a link-in-bio page for free in a few minutes: choose a free builder, claim a username, add your links, and publish. You'll get a single URL (like `lin.ky/yourname`) that you paste into your Instagram, TikTok, X, or YouTube bio.

[Linky](/i/pricing) is free to start - no credit card required. Here's the whole process.

## Step by step

1. **Claim your username.** Pick the handle you want for your page, e.g. `lin.ky/yourname`.
2. **Add your links and blocks.** Drag in a link for your shop, your latest video, your newsletter, and anything else. You can also add live blocks for [Spotify](/i/integrations/spotify), [Instagram](/i/integrations/instagram), [TikTok](/i/integrations/tiktok), and more.
3. **Pick a theme.** Start from a [template](/i/templates) and tweak the colours and fonts to match your brand.
4. **Publish and share.** Copy your URL and paste it into the website/link field of each social profile.

## What you get on the free plan

A free Linky page includes your own page, multiple blocks, custom themes, and the core integrations. If you later want a [custom domain](/i/learn/can-i-use-my-own-domain), analytics, or unlimited blocks, you can upgrade - but you never have to pay to get a polished page live.
