LinkedIn gives you a few places to add a link. On a personal profile, open **Edit intro** and add a website under **Contact info**, or pin a link to the top of your profile using the **Featured** section. On a LinkedIn **Page**, add your URL in the **Website** field under **Edit page**, and consider adding a custom button.

Because each of these spots holds a single link, a link-in-bio page is the easiest way to share more than one thing - your portfolio, your latest post, a booking link, a newsletter - from one URL that you can reorder any time.

## Step by step

1. Build a free link-in-bio page and add a block for each destination.
2. Copy your URL, e.g. `lin.ky/yourname`.
3. On LinkedIn, add it under **Contact info**, the **Featured** section, or your Page's **Website** field.

The same link works across LinkedIn, Instagram, X, and your email signature, so you only ever maintain one place. Start [free](/i/pricing) and pick a [template](/i/templates) that looks professional out of the box.
