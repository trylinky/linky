Twitch gives you two main places for links. Open your **Channel settings** and add entries under **Social links** (these show on your channel under your name), and use **panels** below your stream to add larger, clickable link blocks with images and descriptions.

If you want viewers to find your schedule, merch, Discord, and other socials without cluttering your channel with panels, point one link at a link-in-bio page. It keeps everything in one tidy place you can update any time.

## Step by step

1. Build a free link-in-bio page with a block for each destination - merch, Discord, socials, donations.
2. Copy your URL, e.g. `lin.ky/yourname`.
3. In Twitch, add it under **Social links** and/or as a **panel** beneath your stream.

A link-in-bio page is also the same link you can use on your other platforms, so your community finds the same hub everywhere. Streamers can show live blocks too - like a [Spotify "now playing"](/i/learn/how-to-add-spotify-to-your-link-in-bio) or [GitHub activity](/i/integrations/github). Start [free](/i/pricing).
