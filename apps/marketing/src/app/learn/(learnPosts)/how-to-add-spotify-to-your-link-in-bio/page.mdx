To add Spotify to your link in bio with Linky, add a **Spotify block** to your page and connect your account. The block then shows a live "Now Playing" card with the track you're currently or most recently listening to - and it updates on its own as your listening changes.

This is more engaging than a plain link to your profile: visitors see real, current activity instead of a static button, which makes your page feel alive.

## Step by step

1. Open your Linky page in the editor and add a **Spotify** block.
2. Connect your Spotify account when prompted.
3. Publish - your page now shows your live listening activity.

You can combine it with other [live blocks](/i/integrations) - your [latest Instagram post](/i/integrations/instagram), [TikTok](/i/integrations/tiktok), or [GitHub activity](/i/integrations/github) - so the whole page stays fresh automatically.
